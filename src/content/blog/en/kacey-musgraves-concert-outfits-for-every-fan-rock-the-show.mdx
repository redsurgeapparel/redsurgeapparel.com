## The Classic Country Look: Honoring Her Roots

Kacey Musgraves has always been rooted in country, even though her sound now stretches across multiple genres. Paying homage to her country roots in your outfit can be a fun way to connect with her music and the energy of the concert.

Think denim, cowboy boots, and a bit of fringe. A denim jacket over a simple graphic tee with a pair of skinny jeans or high-waisted shorts gives off that effortlessly cool vibe. It’s a look that says, “I love country music, but I’m here to have fun, too.”

Cowboy boots are a must-have if you’re going for this classic look. Not only are they stylish, but they’re surprisingly comfortable for standing and dancing for hours. Pair them with a wide-brimmed hat, and you’ll have a look that’s festival-ready without overdoing it.

I remember when I went to my first Kacey Musgraves concert back in 2019 during her _[Oh, What a World Tour](https://variety.com/2018/music/news/kacey-musgraves-announces-oh-what-a-world-tour-dates-1202874435/)_. I was kind of torn between dressing casual or going full country. At the last minute, I pulled out my trusty cowboy boots that I’d worn to a few rodeos and paired them with a vintage Willie Nelson tee. I wasn’t sure if I was overdoing the cowboy vibe, but once I got there, I realized how diverse everyone’s style was. Some fans were in rhinestone jackets and cowboy hats, while others were in casual streetwear. It made me realize that a Kacey Musgraves concert is really a place where you can express yourself and feel good doing it.

## Glitz and Glam: Channel Your Inner Star

If you’ve ever seen Kacey Musgraves perform, you know she loves her glitter, sequins, and all things glamorous. Her outfits are often a mix of sparkly retro looks with a modern twist. Why not channel some of that energy with your own concert outfit?

Go for a sequined jacket or a glittery top paired with some sleek black pants or a leather skirt. You can also add some statement accessories like big hoop earrings or a sparkly belt to really shine. Metallic heels or booties will add that extra bit of glam, but make sure they’re comfy enough for a night of standing and dancing.

For a concert at a big arena or outdoor venue, layering is key. You’ll want to be prepared for varying temperatures, so bring a stylish jacket that complements your outfit rather than taking away from it. [A metallic bomber jacket](https://www.whowhatwear.com/styling-bomber-jackets) or faux-fur coat could be the perfect piece to keep you warm while looking fab.

## Retro Vibes: Throwback to the '70s

Kacey Musgraves often channels retro, ['70s-inspired looks](/cyndi-lauper-concert-outfit-what-should-you-wear), from bell-bottoms to floral prints. If you want to embrace that aesthetic, this concert could be the perfect excuse to break out your inner vintage rock star.

Flared jeans, platform shoes, and a fun graphic tee or crop top can give off those nostalgic vibes while keeping you comfortable. Look for flowy fabrics and earthy tones—think rust orange, mustard yellow, and deep greens. You can even take it a step further with oversized sunglasses or a flower crown for a playful festival look.

Last year, I decided to really lean into the retro vibe for Kacey’s concert. I found these amazing bell-bottom jeans at a thrift store and paired them with a flowy floral top that I swear was straight out of 1975. The whole night, I got compliments from other concert-goers, and I even caught the eye of the photographer when I was dancing in the crowd. I felt like I was in some dreamy ‘70s music video. Plus, those bell-bottoms were way more comfortable than I expected—who knew wide-legged jeans would be perfect for dancing?

## Boho Chic: Free-Spirited and Laidback

Kacey’s music often has a dreamy, ethereal quality to it, and a boho-chic outfit would fit right in with the vibe of her shows. Think flowy dresses, lace tops, and earthy accessories. It’s all about looking effortless yet put-together, like you just walked out of a meadow after listening to some Fleetwood Mac.

[Maxi dresses with floral prints](/trendy-floral-print-easter-skirts-spring-into-style-discover-the-hottest-trends) or peasant tops paired with [distressed denim shorts](/distress-denim-shorts-without-bleach-bleach-free-techniques) can give off that free-spirited look. Sandals or ankle boots with fringe can complete the outfit. If you want to add a little extra flair, throw on a floppy hat or some layered necklaces.

What’s great about the boho look is that it’s super comfortable and perfect for an outdoor concert, especially if you’ll be standing or dancing for a long time. You’ll stay cool, but still look amazing.

## Casual and Cool: Keeping It Simple

Maybe you’re not into sequins, fringe, or bell-bottoms, and that’s okay! There’s always the option to keep things simple with a laidback, casual look. At the end of the day, the most important thing is that you’re comfortable and able to enjoy the show.

A classic band tee or a plain white t-shirt paired with distressed jeans and sneakers can be the perfect low-maintenance outfit. Add a leather jacket or a denim jacket to give it a bit of edge, and you’re good to go. This look is all about minimal effort, maximum comfort.

I’ve had my fair share of concert outfit fails, but one time I just kept it super simple with jeans, a t-shirt, and my favorite sneakers. I figured, why overcomplicate it? It was honestly the best decision. I was able to move around easily, dance all night, and I didn’t feel like I was trying too hard. Plus, I still felt like myself, which is probably the most important part of putting together a concert outfit.

## Final Tips for a Kacey Musgraves Concert

*   **Comfort is key**: You’re going to be standing, dancing, and possibly walking a lot. Make sure whatever you’re wearing is comfortable enough for the entire night.
*   **Layer up**: Depending on the venue, you may experience temperature changes, so bring a jacket or sweater that works with your outfit.
*   **Have fun with it**: Don’t be afraid to experiment with your style. Whether you go full-glam or keep it casual, make sure you feel good in what you’re wearing.
*   **Accessorize smartly**: While accessories can elevate your outfit, remember to keep it functional. You don’t want to be stuck carrying around a heavy bag or worrying about a hat flying off during the concert.

## Conclusion

Whether you’re embracing Kacey’s country roots or channeling her glam side, the key to picking the perfect Kacey Musgraves concert outfit is to have fun with it. These shows are a celebration of music, self-expression, and style, so let your personality shine through.
